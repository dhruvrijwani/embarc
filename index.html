Next Steps

Recommendations for Series 2

1
Scale India Students Ad Set
The "Students Interested in International Education" India ad set delivered the best CPL at ₹599. For Series 2, increase budget allocation to this ad set by 30–40% and test 2–3 creative variants to reduce fatigue.
2
Pause or Overhaul SG Students Creative
The Singapore students ad set generated zero leads on ₹9,247 spend. Either pause entirely or develop a localised creative with SG-specific hooks (NUS, NTU pathways, Singapore PR angle) before reactivating.
3
Add Day-of WhatsApp & T-1hr Reminder
The 22.7% show-up rate can be improved with a targeted WhatsApp push at T-1 hour on webinar day. Given 62.6% read rates, this channel is proven effective and can drive an additional 10–15 live attendees.
4
Launch On-Demand Replay Campaign
~77% of signups did not attend live. A 48-hour replay email + WA sequence immediately post-webinar can re-engage this audience and drive them toward a consultation booking with the Embarc team.
5
Improve Email List Hygiene
With 15 bounces across 431 emails (3.5% bounce rate), running a list verification pass before Series 2 will improve deliverability and inbox placement, further boosting open rates above the current 34.6%.
6
Add UTM Tracking to Form
Implementing UTM parameters on all ad creatives and tracking form submissions by source will allow precise attribution — currently India vs SG attribution relies on campaign data, not on-page analytics.
